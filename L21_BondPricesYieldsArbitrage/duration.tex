The duration is give by
\begin{equation}
	D(y)=\sum_{t=1}^{T}{\frac{c/p(y)}{(1+y)^{t}}+\frac{f/p(y)}{(1+y)^{T}}}
\end{equation}
Consider a zero-coupon bond with term $T'$, price $p(y)$ and future value. Then
\begin{equation}
p(y)=\frac{1}{(1+y)^{D(y)}}
\end{equation}

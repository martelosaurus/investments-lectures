\noindent On 2020-03-31, AAPL traded for 273.36 USD. In one month, AAPL can either go up to 300.7 USD or down to 246.02 USD. Suppose that on 2020-03-31, you went short a 248.2 USD call. What was the option premium that you paid or received? How would you hedge your position?

\vspace{9pt}

\noindent\textbf{Solution.} $\Delta_{c}$ is 0.04, $B_{c}$ is 9.83 USD, and so $C_{0}$ is 1.1 USD. To hedge your position, you should go long 0.04 shares of AAPL and short 9.83 USD in the risk-free asset.

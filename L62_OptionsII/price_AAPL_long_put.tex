\noindent On 2020-03-31, AAPL traded for 273.36 USD. In one month, AAPL can either go up to 300.7 USD or down to 246.02 USD. Suppose that on 2020-03-31, you went long a 248.2 USD put. What was the option premium that you paid or received? How would you hedge your position?

\vspace{9pt}

\noindent\textbf{Solution.} $\Delta_{p}$ is 0.96, $B_{p}$ is 288.38 USD, and so $P_{0}$ is 25.95 USD. To hedge your position, you should go short 0.96 shares of AAPL and long 288.38 USD in the risk-free asset.
